Philosophy

Predictability
over generality.

Most systems don't need unbounded queues or dynamic allocation. They need predictable, bounded, specialized primitives that do one thing well and never surprise you at runtime.

SPSC over MPMC
Pay only for the synchronization you actually need
Pre-allocation
Allocate at startup — never on the hot path
Specialization
A conflation slot is not just “a queue of size 1”
Modular by design
